ISKCON Dwarka Dham (Sri Sri Rukminidevi Dwarkadhish Dham) was established by HH Mahavishnu Goswami Maharaj by his pure devotion to Lord Dwarkadhish. He came here in 1996 and set down to study Srila Prabhupada's Bhagavatam. He desired that there should be a nice solid stone temple here in Dwarka.
